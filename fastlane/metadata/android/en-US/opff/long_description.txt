Scan pet food products or their barcode to obtain data on ingredients, additives and nutrition facts.

The app allows to view the over 600 pet food products already contained in the free and open database Applications of pet food, and also to add pictures and data for missing products.

To discover the Applications of pet food project, please visit https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org

***Contribute to Pet Food Transparency***

- Applications of pet food is a pet food products database made by everyone, for everyone.
- You can use it to make better pet food choices, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.
- Applications of pet food is a non-profit project developed by thousands of volunteers from around the world. We have lots of exciting projects you can contribute to in many different ways.
- You can start contributing today by adding a product from your pet's cupboard. 


*** Get the Facts ***

- It works with dog food (dry dog food & wet dog food), cat food, rabbit food, snake food, fish food, bird food, horse food or any food for your favorite animal.

*** Works well in any condition ***
- You can add new products, even if you don't have an Internet connection right now.
- The app loads only the strict necessary to reduce data and battery consumption
- You don't need a camera to look up a product. You can also type the barcode if you lack a camera or have an issue with it.

*** Your pet food, your data ***
- Your personal data is yours, and is never sent online
- You can use the app anonymously
- You can export your scan history


For human food, you can install Application of food products, for beauty, Application of cosmetic products, and for all other products, Open Products Facts.
